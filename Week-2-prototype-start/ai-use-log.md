# Summary of AI Usage

AI tools were used throughout the project to assist with brainstorming, research, coding support, accessibility ideas, presentation preparation, and documentation organization. The group used AI responsibly as a supporting tool rather than relying on it to complete the project automatically. All outputs generated by AI were reviewed, edited, tested, and improved manually before inclusion in the final submission.

---

# Limitations Encountered While Using AI

* Some generated suggestions were too advanced for the target audience
* AI-generated coding suggestions occasionally contained errors
* Generated layouts sometimes required redesigning for responsiveness
* Human testing was required to verify usability and accessibility
* Multiple revisions were necessary to align outputs with coursework requirements

---

# Ethical and Responsible Use of AI

The group used AI ethically to support productivity, learning, idea generation, and organization. AI was not used to replace understanding or independent development work. All AI-generated content was critically reviewed, manually modified, and tested before implementation. The group ensured transparency by documenting all AI-assisted activities within this log.
