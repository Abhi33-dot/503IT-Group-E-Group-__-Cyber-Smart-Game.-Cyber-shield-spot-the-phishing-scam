️ Stay Safe Online!

ARIA's top tips to avoid phishing scams

  1. Check the Sender

    Always look at who sent the message. Real companies use their official email domain (like @amazon.com). Scammers use weird addresses like @amaz0n-deals.net.

  2. ⏰

    Watch Out for Urgency

    "Act NOW or lose everything!" Scammers create panic to stop you from thinking. Real messages are never that urgent. Take a deep breath and think first.

  3. Too Good to Be True?

    Free prizes, huge rewards, and giveaways you didn't enter are almost always scams. If it sounds too amazing, it's probably fake!

  4. Don't Click Unknown Links

    Never click links in unexpected messages. Instead, go directly to the official website by typing it in your browser yourself.

  5. Never Share Passwords

    No real company will ever ask for your password, PIN, or full bank details by email, text, or phone. Ever. Full stop.

  6. ‍‍

    Tell a Trusted Adult

    If something feels weird or scary online, always tell a parent, guardian, or teacher. You won't get in trouble – it's the right thing to do!

  7. QR Codes Can Be Fakes

    Scammers put fake QR codes in public places. Only scan QR codes from sources you fully trust – and always check the URL before opening anything.
